Please note the following upcoming changes to the We Financial Cardholder agreement for your We Financial® Visa* Prepaid Card, which will take effect on August 1, 2025.

Funds loaded onto the card are held by the issuer Peoples Trust Company, a member institution of the CDIC, and are eligible for CDIC coverage. We Financial is not a CDIC member institution.
